# Cover prompt

Create a 16:9 editorial cover on warm cream paper in hand-drawn sketch-note style. A stream of colorful message cards enters a wide event highway split into parallel numbered lanes, travels through a compact cluster of broker towers, and reaches several consumer teams. Use navy ink, Kafka-inspired black, coral, teal and warm yellow. Exact title only: “Kafka, Made Simple”. Large handwritten title, balanced mood, generous whitespace, no subtitle, no brand logo, no dense labels, no photorealism.
