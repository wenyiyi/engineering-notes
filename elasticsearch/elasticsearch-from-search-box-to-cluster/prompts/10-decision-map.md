# Decision map

Create a 16:9 hand-drawn decision map titled “Do I need Elasticsearch?”. Start with three visual questions: fuzzy/full-text search, relevance/highlight, many searchable dimensions at scale. “Yes” paths lead to Elasticsearch. Exact primary-key lookup, strong multi-row transactions, and tiny datasets lead to MySQL or a simpler solution. End with a rule card: “Search projection, not source of truth.” Minimal English labels, no paragraph text. Warm off-white paper, navy sketch lines, teal positive paths, coral caution paths.
