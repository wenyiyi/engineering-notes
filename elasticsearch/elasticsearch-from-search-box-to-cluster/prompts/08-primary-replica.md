# Primary and replica

Create a 16:9 hand-drawn reliability diagram. Three nodes hold shard boxes: P0 with R0 on another node, P1 with R1 elsewhere. One node fails with a harmless unplugged-cable icon; a replica is promoted and traffic continues. Show the cluster changing from green to yellow during recovery and back to green after a new replica is allocated. Minimal labels: Primary, Replica, Failover, Recover. Avoid implying replicas are backups stored on the same node. Warm off-white background, navy, teal, coral.
